Card Counter

This game is inspired by the idea of counting cards, which is done to increase your odds of winning a luck based card game. You can choose a card from any of the four decks. Choosing a red card increases the number based on the number of the card, and choosing a blue card decreases it. The objective is to get through all of the cards without going below zero or above 20.
