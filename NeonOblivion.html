Corruption Shader

Neon Oblivion is about a medieval world that is slowly being corrupted by magic, slowly transitioning to a vaporwave style. It's a bit goofy, but was a fun theme, and I created the 'Vaporwave Corruption' shader that we used all over the game to show this transition. I originally coded the shader in HLSL but transitioned it to use Unity's shader graph.
